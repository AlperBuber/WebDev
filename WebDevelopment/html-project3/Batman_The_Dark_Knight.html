Batman The Dark Knight

Summary

Directed by Christopher Nolan | Sequel to "Batman Begins" (2005) Gotham City is experiencing a decline in organized crime thanks to Batman, District Attorney Harvey Dent, and Lieutenant Jim Gordon. However, a new, chaotic villain known as the Joker emerges, upending Gotham’s fragile sense of order. The Joker begins a campaign of terror, robbing banks and assassinating key officials while pushing Batman to his moral limits. He orchestrates a devastating attack that kills Gotham’s police commissioner and a judge. When he targets Harvey Dent, Bruce Wayne’s childhood love, Rachel Dawes, is also put at risk. Bruce considers revealing his identity as Batman, but Harvey pretends to be Batman to draw out the Joker. However, Joker attacks and captures both Harvey and Rachel, placing them in separate locations rigged with explosives. Batman tries to save Rachel but is misled by the Joker; instead, he finds Harvey, who survives but is horribly disfigured, becoming Two-Face
